Janis Amy Reynolds

Location: New York, NY

Specialty: PTSD

I started my professional career providing mental health therapy to clients in 2016. I have Bachelors and Masters degrees in Education as well as a Masters degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Canisius College. I have a client-centered approach and use cognitive behavioral therapy, solution focused therapy, and motivational interviewing. My care philosophy involves using the body and verbally processing to identify and regulate emotions. Healing and re-learning is difficult, but possible with care, buildable tools, insights and brave action.
